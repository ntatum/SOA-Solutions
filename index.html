Wealth Architecture

Your Roadmap to Abundance

Phase-by-phase strategies built for real people starting from where they are — not where the finance industry assumes they should be.

Phase 1 — Foundation
Stabilize & Protect

Build your $1,000 starter emergency fund. Eliminate high-interest debt using the debt snowball. Open your first HYSA. Establish a spending plan aligned with your values.

Emergency Fund Debt Elimination Budgeting
Phase 2 — Growth
Invest & Multiply

Max your employer 401k match. Open a Roth IRA. Begin index fund investing. Explore real estate via REITs or your first rental property. Build 3–6 month reserves.

Roth IRA Index Funds Real Estate
Phase 3 — Enterprise
Build Your Business

Launch or scale your income-producing business. Separate business & personal finances. Use business credit to leverage. Create systems that generate income without your time.

Business Credit Cash Flow Systems
